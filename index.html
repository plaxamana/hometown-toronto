Top three activities to do in Toronto

See Toronto from the top of the CN Tower

The CN Tower, also known as the Canadian National Tower, was the world's tallest freestanding structure until 2007. See the beautiful views of the city from 1,815 feet (553 metres) in the sky!

Take a museum tour at the Royal Ontario Museum

Spectacular Displays of Art, Culture, and Nature in the Heart of Toronto. Home to a Collection of 13 Million Artworks, Cultural Objects & Natural History Specimens.

Sea life at Ripley's Aquarium

Visit Ripley's Aquarium and immerse yourself in a world of 20000 aquatic animals and discover your own underwater adventure at Ripley's Aquarium of Canada!
